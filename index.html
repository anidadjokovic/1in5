1 in 5 Women Will Experience Sexual Assault

By Anida Djokovic

This website is dedicated to different types of information related to sexual assault

Statistics related to sexual assault

-Females between the ages of 16-19 are 4 times more likely to be victims of sexual assault, rape, or attempted rape

-91% of sexual assault victims are females, while 9% are male victims

-In 8 out of 10 cases of rape the victim knew their attacker

-321,500 victims on average a year of sexual assault that is 12 or older

-Every 98 seconds an American is sexually assaulted

What to do if you or someone you know has been sexually assaulted

Safety is the most important. Are you or the person safe? If not call 911 right away

What happened was not your or their fault. Something happened that you or them didn't want to happen and that is NOT OK

Call the National Sexual Assault Hotline. A staff member will direct you to the appropriate health facility for survivors

Hot lines to call:

RAINN AKA Rape, Abuse and Incest National Network: 800-656-HOPE(4673)

VictimConnect: 1-855-4-VICTIM(1-855-484-2846)

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK(8255)
